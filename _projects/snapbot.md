Snapbot is a manipulator-based computational photography system that tracks people and selects camera poses for dynamic human-robot interaction.

- Integrated perception, control, and computational photography into an end-to-end HRI system.
- Estimated relative transform between the detected person and the robot.
- Implemented inverse-kinematics closed-loop manipulator control with filtering and shaped pseudo-inverse singularity avoidance.
- Contributed to the ACM/IEEE HRI 2024 publication listed on the publications page.
